New listings, scored by how likely they are to hurt you. This ranks by risk, lowest first — it is not a list of picks.
Read this before using it. Most new tokens lose their holders money, and a meaningful share are built to. This scanner checks the patterns that precede losses — shallow liquidity, blocked sells, unbacked supply, paid promotion, vertical price moves. It catches common rugs. It cannot see a contract that has not rugged yet, and no scanner can. A low risk score means fewer red flags, never "safe" and never a recommendation.
